1. Introduction: Why This Matters
In today's digital landscape, the integrity of our corporate network is paramount. Firewalls serve as our first line of defense, monitoring and controlling incoming and outgoing network traffic based on predetermined security rules.
Without a robust understanding and implementation of these security measures, we expose our organization to significant risks, including:
	Data Breaches: Loss of sensitive client or proprietary data.
	Unauthorized Access: Malicious actors gaining entry to our internal systems.
Operational Downtime: Disruption of business continuity due to cyberattacks.

This workshop aims to equip our team with the knowledge required to maintain a secure digital perimeter.
